Since 1962 the Golf Farm in Voorhees Township, was a family favorite for mini-golf and then 18-hole Pitch & Putt. It was set to permanently close and was shut down in March, 2025.

Last week we were excited to learn that the business will reopen under their new name, Voorhees Golf Lane. It will roll out the Mini-golf first and then add the Pitch & Putt as soon as the course is ready.
